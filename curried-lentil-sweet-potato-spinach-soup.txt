Curried lentil, sweet potato, & spinach soup

In large pot, saute in olive oil 1 diced yellow onion and 1 diced red onion
Add 1 bulb minced garlic (tube of puree is fastest)
Add 1 2-3 inch hunk of minced ginger (tube of puree is fastest)
Add Â¼ tsp red pepper flakes
Add 1 Tbs each ground turmeric, ground coriander, and curry powder
Add 1 small can (6 oz?) tomato paste
Continue to saute/stir for a couple minutes
Add 4-6 cups vegetable stock 
Add 2 (14 oz) cans coconut milk
Add 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
Add 1.5 cups red lentils, bring to boil and simmer 15-20 minutes
Add 2-3 tsp salt (to taste)
Add 2 peeled/diced sweet potatoes and simmer for 15 minutes (until tender)
Add juice of ~2 limes (to taste)
Before serving, add a big bunch of spinach and stir it in
Serve with cilantro and lime wedges
